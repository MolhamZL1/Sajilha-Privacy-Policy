This Privacy Policy describes how the Sajelha App ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by the Sajelha App Team, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us during registration, including:

We use the collected information to:

All data is stored securely on our private server. We implement encryption and secure communication (HTTPS) to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers such as Google Analytics for the purpose of app usage analysis and improvement.

We do not sell, rent, or disclose user data to other third parties. We also do not provide data to legal authorities unless required by law.

5. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
This App is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. If we discover that data has been collected from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete such data promptly.

6. DEVICE PERMISSIONS
The App does not request access to your camera, location, microphone, or any other device permissions.

7. DATA RETENTION
We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App’s services.
